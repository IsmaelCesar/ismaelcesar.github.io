Research projects I worked on

2018 - 2019 - Quantum Machine Learning: Models and Learning algorithms

Coordinator: Adenilton José da Silva
This work investigates and evaluates the use of quantum associative memories and other quantum algorithms to encode data in the basis states for quantum neural networks. In my participation I also contributed with the implementation a simulation of quantum ensembles composed by quantum nural networks.

2016 - (in progress) - Quantum Neural Networks: Models and Learning algorithms and architecture selection

Coordinator: Adenilton José da Silva
This work studies the use of quantum associative memories in the training, architecture selection and performance evaluation of quantum neural networks. In addition, I also contributed to the implementation of procedures that generate quantum circuits for data encoding in quantum state.
